Fanpage : Bloodborne

Statistics

Level: Your character's current level, also affects your physical defense.

Strength: Boosts trick weapon damage. Each point boosts Strength-based weapons.

Skill: Increases trick weapon damage, boosting Skill-based weapons, and raising your skill above 50 increases the damage of visceral attacks.

Arcane: Affects the power of Arcane attack, although it doesn't boost physical damage, and increases discovery rates.

Bloodtinge: Determines whether or not you can use certain weapons and affects weapons that deal Blood damage.

Vitality: Your character's HP

Endurance: Deals with your character's stamina and resitances.

Insight: Currency that represent's the depth of your character's knowledge that can be obtained by slaying bosses, discovering areas, speaking to NPCS, or consuming Madman's Knowledge. This currency can also help the player character trigger certain events based on how much insight they have.

Blood Echoes: Main currency in Bloodborne received by killing enemies, consuming Coldblood, or selling items.
